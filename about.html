Our Story

Fit for the Kingdom

Fit 4 the Kingdom 365 was born from a simple yet powerful vision: to create a space where physical fitness and spiritual wellness come together. Located in the heart of Blythewood, South Carolina, we've built more than just a gym – we've created a community.

Our founders believed that true wellness encompasses the whole person – body, mind, and spirit. This belief drives everything we do, from our training programs to the welcoming atmosphere you'll experience the moment you walk through our doors.

Whether you're beginning your fitness journey or looking to take it to the next level, you'll find a supportive community here that celebrates every step forward.

Our Purpose

Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To provide a welcoming, faith-centered environment where individuals and families can strengthen their bodies, refresh their minds, and nourish their spirits through fitness, fellowship, and community.

Our Vision

To be Blythewood's premier destination for holistic wellness – a place where every person, regardless of fitness level, feels empowered to pursue their best self in body, mind, and spirit.
